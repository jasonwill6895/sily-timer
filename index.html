This service is developed and maintained by the Sily Timer Project Team (Origin Logic). We value your privacy; this policy explains how we handle information.

1. 資訊收集與使用 | Information Collection

Sily Timer 是一款純本地運作的工具。我們不會收集、上傳或傳輸任何個人資料。
Sily Timer is a local-only tool. We do not collect, upload, or transmit any personal data.

  • 計時數據 (Timer Data)：僅儲存在您裝置的 Local Storage 中。
    Stored only in your device's local storage.
  • 權限說明 (Permissions)：若請求通知權限，僅用於計時結束時的提醒。
    Notification permissions are used only for timer completion alerts.

2. 數據儲存 | Data Storage

所有偏好設定（如專注時長、能量點數）均透過標準本地存儲技術保留在您的裝置上。刪除應用程式將同步移除這些數據。
All preferences (e.g., focus duration, energy points) are kept on your device via standard local storage. Deleting the app will remove this data.
